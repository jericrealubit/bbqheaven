Ordering Terms

Please read these terms carefully before placing your order.

01. Order Fulfillment

All orders are subject to availability. Because our BBQ is cooked "Low and Slow" for up to 12 hours, certain items (like Brisket or Ribs) may sell out. We reserve the right to contact you and offer a substitution or a refund if an item becomes unavailable after your order is placed.

02. Pickup & Quality

Orders must be collected from 6/33-39 Pinjarra Rd, Mandurah. To ensure the best quality, we recommend picking up your food at the exact time specified in your confirmation. BBQ Heaven is not responsible for the food quality if collected more than 20 minutes after the scheduled time.

03. Payments & Refunds

Payments are processed securely via our ordering partner, GloriaFood. Refunds are only issued if:

  • The order is cancelled by the restaurant.
  • Items are missing from your order and cannot be replaced.
  • There is a significant quality issue reported within 1 hour of pickup.

04. Alcohol Policy

If your order includes alcoholic beverages, you must be 18 years of age or older. We operate under strict RSA (Responsible Service of Alcohol) guidelines. Valid photo ID must be presented upon collection. If ID cannot be produced, the alcohol will be removed from the order and a refund for that portion will be issued.
